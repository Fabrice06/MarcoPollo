MarcoPolo

The application MarcoPolo is the solution to manage your internet bookmarks . It is simple and easy to use. You can record and find your favorite pages . The bookmarks will be ranked according to your choice. Discover your application , navigate ... . Have a nice trip !


Home

This screen allows the user to connect to the site or to access different information links .

  1. By default , the site MarcoPolo is in French. Click on " French " or " English " allows to choose the language of the site.
  2. Enter their email address and password in the text fields to identify
  3. Click on " Connect " to access his bookmarks.
  4. A click on the link " Do not have an account? " Sends the page
  5. A click on the link "I forgot my password ! "Allows the user to get back on his email address password .
  6. Clicking on the " Who are we? "Provides the user with information on our MarcoPolo group.
  7. A click on the "Contact Us" allows the user to contact us.

Update my profile

This screen allows the user to update his demettre email or unsubscribe from the site.

  1. Click on the "Logout" link allows you to disconnect from the site.
  2. Enter a new email address can change their user profile .
  3. Click "Cancel" to return to the previous page without making any changes .
  4. Click on "Delete" deletes the email address and therefore the user can not connect .... ???
  5. Click "Edit" to validate the change.

Bookmarks List

On this screen the user views the list of bookmarks. It can add , delete , modify or directly access the internet page of the bookmark .

  1. Field "Search" allows you to search the list of names and page links.
  2. Click on "Add" allows you to create a page bookmark.
  3. Click the end of the line delete button deletes the corresponding mark - page .
  4. Click the end of the line edit button to edit the corresponding page - mark .
  5. Click on the link for the bookmark allows access to the web page corresponding to the bookmark.

New bookmark

On this screen the user creates a new bookmark . He must choose a name and a link to it.

  1. Click on "Create" allows access to the edit page of a bookmark after verification mandatory.

Editing Bookmark

This screen allows you to edit a bookmark . You can change the name and the link but also add a tag ( key set value ) in order to characterize more precisely .

  1. Click "Edit" to validate the change made after verification mandatory.
  2. Click "Delete" to erase a Submit Thread in his intégralitée .
  3. Click "Cancel" to return to his Submit Thread list.
  4. Click on "+" to create a new tag attached to Submit Thread (both fields are required) .
  5. Click "x" to remove a tag .
